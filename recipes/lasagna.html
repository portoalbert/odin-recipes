This is a recipe I learned from my great grandparents. I always make it when people are over and it's a total sucess!

If you ever want to impress that special someone try this on your own!

Ingredients

  • 187 Lasagna noodles
  • Fresh tomatoes from japan
  • Rigawt
  • Water from mississipi river
  • love

Directions

  1. Preheat microwave to 420
  2. Boil Lasagna noodles for 2 hours or until noodle has rubber like consistency
  3. In a separe bowl combine rigawt, tomatoes and love
  4. Microwave everything for 32 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
